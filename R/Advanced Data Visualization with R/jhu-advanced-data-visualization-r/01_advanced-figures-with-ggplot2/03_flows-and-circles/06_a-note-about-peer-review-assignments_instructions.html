O​ur course is set up so that required Peer Review assignments have three "required" reviews. However, sometimes reviews are a little slower to come in. If that happens, don't worry. You are able to pass the course with just one required review, assuming you have reviewed three of your colleague's assignments.

H​ere is more information about the grading for peer review assignments:

To receive your grade on a peer-graded assignment:

  • You must submit your assignment

  • You must review a specified number of peers’ assignments (for this course, that number is three).

  • 1 or more peers must review your assignment, in order to get your grade

  • If you submitted your assignment on or before the deadline, you’ll get your grade within  7-10 days if 1 or more of your peers reviewed your submission

  • If you submitted your assignment after the deadline, you’ll only get your grade after ALL of the required peers review your assignment

  • Keep in mind that it can take up to 7-10 days for your assignment to be graded by other learners
